  • Every time money is pulled from the register, record a Paid Out for a clean paper trail.

  • Section 2 auto-creates payout periods (unique team + contiguous time). Any period < 5 minutes is merged into an adjacent period.
  • Enter Cash/Card for each period, then Calculate. Quick Totals show first; open the Audit for the math.
