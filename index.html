Barista Tip Splitter

How To Use
  • Every time money is pulled from the register, it must be Paid Out for a clean paper trail.
  • Tips are split evenly among people actively working windows and making drinks during that time.
  • Not eligible: anyone off-shift and just staying to clean.
  • Add each barista once, then add one or more shifts under that barista.
  • Section 2 auto-creates payout periods (unique team + contiguous time). Enter the Cash and Card Paid Out for each line.
  • Use Smart rounding to make totals match inputs to the cent.
  • The Audit shows who split what for each period.
